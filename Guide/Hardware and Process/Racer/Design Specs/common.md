# List of design specifications

- The VTX and other parts that requires calling must be exposed from the body.

- The antennas used for log distance operation like ELRS and VTX must be exposed outside the body.

- The external antennas must be fitted with 3D printed parts with airfoil to minimize air drag.

## Notion
- Tilt is always defined from Zenith. A tilt of zero means the object is pointing at Zenith.

## Fasteners
- The nuts, when used, must all have thread lock applied.
- Use heat insert whenever possible.
- Do not use loose nuts that would fall down when a bolt is unscrewed.
