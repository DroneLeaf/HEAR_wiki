# Features

- Supports launching of 5-inch drones with pan/tilt mechanism
- Supports launching of 7-inch drones with static fix

# Design specifications for launcher V0

## Drone attachment to the launcher
- The carbon rods plugged into the launcher are fixed and not easy to remove.
- The carbon rods attached to the drones are cut to length, firm to position, and have end caps to prevent the rods from passing through the end caps.
